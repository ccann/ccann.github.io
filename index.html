Cody Canning

In May 2013 I received a Masters degree in computer science from Tufts University where I was a research assistant in the Human-Robot Interaction lab under Dr. Matthias Scheutz. In October of 2013 I decided to leave Tufts and the Ph.D. program to pursue career opportunities in industry.

My research publications include investiations into human perceptions of robots in various domains (e.g. remote team tasks, task demonstration) and the utility of fNIRS for passive brain-computer interfaces.

Please feel free to contact me about job opportunities or with inquiries about my research.
